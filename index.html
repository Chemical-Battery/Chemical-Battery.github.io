About Me

Hi. I’m Martha Berkes, and I’m so glad you’re here! I started my company, Red Bird Review, following a long and successful career as a print and broadcast Journalist, Professional Writer/Editor, and Public Relations Specialist. My first clients were first-time book authors, and after helping them expand and grow their visibility and sales, and in some cases, publish their first books, I knew my calling was to operate my own business. In addition to my author clients, I have provided my exceptional writing and editing skills for those needing resumes, cover letters, business plans, and editing of books, and most recently, screenplays for films! Whatever your needs or projects, Red Bird Review is your one-stop shop for marketing, public relations, media relations, and all forms of communication. I look forward to serving your needs, and using my success to make you a success!

Working With Me

I value my clients’ time, as I do my own. From our first interaction, to the final touches of your project, you can expect timely responses and attention to detail from me. It all starts with your first contact message, which I will respond to within 48 hours or less. I will personalize a plan of action suited for each client, and keep you updated on the progress we make together. At Red Bird Review, we know that our success and reputation depend on our clients’ total satisfaction!

Pricing

At Red Bird Review, we offer several different pricing options. Short-end projects will be quoted at affordable hourly rates. We also offer monthly contracts with mutually-agreed upon fees. No matter what your project requires, you can count on transparent pricing to fit your budget.
